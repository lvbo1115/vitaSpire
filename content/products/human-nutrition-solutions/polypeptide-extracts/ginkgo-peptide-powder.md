## Product Name

Ginkgo Peptide Powder

## Background

Ginkgo biloba peptide extract is derived from ginkgo biloba fruit. It is rich in easily absorbed small-molecule active peptides. It possesses excellent antioxidant capacity, helping to eliminate free radicals and delay cell aging; it also supports cardiovascular and cerebrovascular health, assists in regulating blood lipids, and exhibits potential neuroprotective and anti-fatigue effects. This product has a small molecular weight and high bioavailability, making it an ideal functional ingredient for enhancing immunity and promoting overall health.

## Product Description

The food-grade raw material is produced using carefully selected ginkgo as the primary ingredient. Through a controlled and modernized manufacturing process, drinking water, proteases, and other approved auxiliary materials are added. This mixture then undergoes a series of refined steps, including enzymatic hydrolysis, filtration, decolorization , concentration, sterilization, spray drying, and final packaging. The result is a consistent, high-quality ingredient suitable for a range of health-focused food and nutraceutical applications.
