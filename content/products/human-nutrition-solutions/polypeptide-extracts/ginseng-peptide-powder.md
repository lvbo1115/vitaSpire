## Cat.

NEPP-Ginseng

## Product Name

Ginseng Peptide Powder

## Background

Ginseng extract peptides are made from high-quality ginseng raw materials and processed through extraction. The product is rich in small-molecule active peptides, which are easily absorbed. It has core functions of enhancing immunity and relieving physical fatigue, while also contributing to antioxidant effects, improving cellular metabolism, and supporting nerve function and stress regulation. This product has high bioavailability and is a high-quality functional ingredient for improving sub-health conditions and enhancing overall vitality.

## Product Description

The food-grade raw material is produced using carefully selected ginsing as the primary ingredient. Through a controlled and modernized manufacturing process, drinking water, proteases, and other approved auxiliary materials are added. This mixture then undergoes a series of refined steps, including enzymatic hydrolysis, filtration, decolorization , concentration, sterilization, spray drying, and final packaging. The result is a consistent, high-quality ingredient suitable for a range of health-focused food and nutraceutical applications.
